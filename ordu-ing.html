  • Yason Cape

    • Cape Yason Peninsula is a peninsula with a natural appearance. It is on the old Army-Samsun Highway. The name of the church on the peninsula, which is within the borders of Çaytepe, is also known as Jason's Church. This church was built by the Greeks and Georgians living in the area in 1868 and its architecture is very characteristic.

  • Gaga Lake

    • Gaga Lake, located 10 km southeast of Fatsa, on the edge of the Fatsa-Aybastı highway, is located within the borders of Sefaköy. The lake has many aquatic plant and animal species diversity as well as its natural beauty. The depth of Gaga Lake, which has an area of 80,000,000 m2, varies between 10-15 m.

  • Walnuts Halva

    • Another dessert recipe that is quite famous in Ordu, where you can see all the shades together, is halva dessert with walnuts. This type of dessert, which is unique to the Ordu region, is a type of dessert that is often preferred not only in this city but also in other provinces of Turkey. Making halva with walnuts is much harder than making some types of sweets. But it's a dessert worth trying and doing. If you are someone who likes to try different desserts and you also like to eat desserts, you should definitely try the halva dessert with walnuts.
