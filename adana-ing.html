    Nature Sports in Adana

    The Central and Eastern Taurus mountains covering the north of the province provide conditions suitable for mountain and hiking. Peaks above 3000 m in elevation are ideal for climbing, while the lower elevations are ideal for hiking. It is possible to reach the peaks by camping on Carnation Mountain near Pozanti. This place is also adjacent to the Chamardi region within the borders of Niğde, which is used extensively for mountain and nature walks. The second suitable area for climbing is the village of Mansurlu, connected to Feke. The area is close to the southern wall of Demirkazik peak. Doğanbeyli near saimbeyli district is another region suitable for mountaineering and hiking.

    Trekking Routes: 1- Starting Pozantı-Hamidiye village -; Mazmuru Mountain, end Niğde-Demirkazık mountain accommodation: Mazmuru locality. 2– Starting Pozanti - Hamidiye Village-Egni suyu (accommodation) - Trak mevkii - Acisu (accommodation) - ending Yedigöze waterfalls or aladag district center. Accommodation: Egni suyu, Acisu locality. 3– Starting Karaisalı district – Karakılıç village – Darıçukuru - Köşk – Damlama-Ekecik Belemedik Village, end bürücek plateau.

    Accommodation: belemedik village.

    Equestrian hiking: route of Pozantı District; end and accommodation of pear groove-Kızıldağ Plateau.

  • Stone Bridge

    • The bridge, which is considered the symbol of Adana, is a Roman era artifact. IV. It was built by the Roman Emperor Hadrianus in the (385) century. It has been an important bridge between Europe and Asia for centuries. .The stone bridge is 319 meters long and 13 meters high. 14 of his 21 belts are standing. Two lion reliefs can be seen in the large arch in the middle. It is known as one of the oldest bridges still in use in the world.

  • Varda German Bridge

    • The Varda Bridge, located about one kilometer south-west of Hacıkırı village in Karaisalı District, in the direction of Adana-Ankara, is called Koca Bridge by the people. The bridge is 99 meters high and 172 meters long, connecting a deep valley. At the foot of the quiet mountains, the Varda bridge, established in the mis-like Forest, welcomes many visitors every year. There are many picnic and sightseeing areas around the Varda Bridge, also known as the German bridge.

  • Şırdan

    • Among the local dishes of Adana, sirdan is also included. Demand for this food made from animal offal is quite high in the city. There are many points where you can taste this offal dish made from part of the animal's stomach. The animal offal usually used is lamb. It is a food served by cooking after adding specially prepared spicy rice to the stuffed food.

  • Bici Bici

    • Among the foods consumed to cool off in the hot summer months, Bici Bici dessert comes first. Although its preparation is very simple, its consumption is quite enjoyable. It is prepared by grating a piece of ice on the custard made using starch.
