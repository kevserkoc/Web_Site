  • Peri Bacaları

    • Although there are fairy chimneys in many parts of Nevşehir, let's note that the most frequent and oldest fairy chimneys are found in Cappadocia. Fairy chimneys, known as the most important symbol of both Cappadocia and Nevşehir, fascinate and amaze those who officially come thanks to their natural formations.

  • Üç Güzeller

    • Three beauties, consisting of two large and one small fairy chimneys, are another symbolic structure of the region. These three beauties, which are one of the most photographed places in the region, attract the attention of many local and foreign tourists with their image and legends told about them.

  • Soğanlama

    • Nevşehir-specific onion is a hearty dish that is not troublesome to make. We recommend that you try this dish, which occurs when coarsely chopped onions are combined with dough, when your way to Nevşehir falls.

  • Stuffed Quince

    • Among the local dishes of Nevşehir, this time a different flavor meets us. Cooked by filling the carved quince with minced meat and wedding, the dish combines both sweet and salty taste with the same flavor.

  • Meatballs

    • A sweet variety made from grapes grown in Nevsehir vineyards. Dessert made with grape juice, starch, flour is sliced and served after drying. Meatballs can be a great alternative to serving them alongside a cup of coffee.

  • Sızgıt

    • It is a cold snack that occurs when small chopped meats are dried after roasting the meat with oil. In order for the meat to be stored for longer, this method is also used. The dish, which appears in other regions under the name roasting, is known as süzgit in Nevşehir.
