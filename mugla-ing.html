  • Creep

    • Muğla cuisine is one of the most delicious tastes of the Black Sea muhlam with the image of sündürme. Creep, which is the first preferred flavor at breakfasts and practical tea hours, attracts attention with its hearty flavor. Despite this, the creep, which is a fairly mild flavor with radish, pomegranate sour and garlic used in its production, is quite delicious and useful.

  • Rice With Octopus

    • One of the most interesting flavors of Muğla is rice with octopus. The flavor prepared by adding octopus to the classic rice always finds its place in the special menus. Octopus rice is an important flavor prepared from seafood, which is a pretty good opportunity for those who want to experience different flavors other than the known classic flavors.

  • Çökertme Kebab

    • Among the local delicacies of Muğla, the name of which is most heard and known in almost every point of our country and eaten with pleasure is a flavor of çökertme kebab. Çökertme kebab, which is the first among the kebab varieties of Muğla cuisine, is made by adding veal with butter sauce on thin French fries. Çökertme kebab, which can also be used as chicken meat instead of veal, is one of the flavors that must be tasted.

  • Sour Fish

    • Because Muğla is the seaside and is one of the most popular places where marine tourism is made in our country, there is a separate place of seafood in Muğla cuisine. Apart from the classic fish flavors, Muğla offers great flavors for those who taste local fish flavors prepared in many different ways.

  • Choker Dessert

    • In Muğla sweet culture, even meat is made sweet. Yeah, you didn't hear wrong. Choker dessert is made from a lamb choker. So it is met with great interest by tourists in the region. Gerdan dessert, which passed from Ottoman cuisine to Muğla cuisine, was called Sultan dessert at the time. Although a little heavy, it has a pretty delicious taste. It is also quite ideal for those who like to try different flavors.
