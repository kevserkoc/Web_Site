  • Güvercinada Castle

    • Güvercinada Castle, located in Hacifeyzullah District of Kuşadası, was built by Barbaros Hayrettin Pasha. Located in a position protecting the port at the mouth of the Gulf of Kusadasi, the castle was built to prevent attacks that may occur during the Peloponnese rebellion.

  • Aydın Archaeological Museum

    • Aydin Archaeological Museum is located in a large garden, which contains stone artifacts from various periods from ancient cities such as Tralleis, Magnesia, Alinda, Alabanda, Nysa, amyzon, Piginda, Harpasa, Myus, Pygela, Orthosia and Mastaura. These are sculptures, sarcophagi, columns and column heads, altars, grave stelae, Islamic tombstones, milestones, inscribed and embossed stelae and various architectural pieces.

  • Milet Museum

    • In the Miletus Museum, which consists of two main parts: garden display and indoor display; artifacts obtained as a result of excavations in the ancient city of Miletus, the Temple of Apollo in Didim and the ancient city of Priene are displayed.

  • Keşkek

    • Keşkek, made with wheat, chicken or lamb meat and butter, reaches the power to feast on the stomachs alone when the sauce prepared with butter and paprika is poured on it. This unique flavor, which shows that one of the most beautiful dishes in the world can be made with few ingredients, is especially served in Aydin on special occasions such as weddings and circumcision.

  • Prairie Fries

    • Eggplants, peppers and tomatoes, which are fried separately in the pan, are placed on a baking sheet. All the whole garlic is cooked in the oven and ready to serve with its exquisite flavor and appearance.

  • Yam Dinner

    • Yams dish, where yams are sweetened with vegetables such as potatoes, onions and carrots, is one of the most well-known local flavors of the Aydin region. Delicious or delicious, but also easy to make.

  • Tahini Pita

    • Intellectuals know that Pita can only be so beautiful in the country. Especially if it's tahini. Advice from us to those who plan to make a trip to Aydin, Soke, Nazilli, most of all, do not skip visiting the pideciler located in Yenipazar. Otherwise, you'll miss the best pita bread you can eat in your life.

  • Dried fig walnuts

    • Aydin's legendary food, dried figs, fresh ones, delicious. If your path falls, you can stay energetic by eating a lot of dried fig walnuts during your trip, and on the way back, you can bring boxes of dried figs to your partner and friend.
